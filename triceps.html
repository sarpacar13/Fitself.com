WELCOME TO TRICEPS WORKOUTS!!

A well-rounded fitness routine should never neglect triceps workouts. The triceps, located on the back of your upper arms, play a crucial role in arm strength and aesthetics. Engaging in triceps exercises helps build muscle definition and tone, contributing to a more balanced physique. Popular triceps exercises include tricep dips, close-grip bench press, tricep pushdowns, skull crushers, and diamond push-ups. These workouts target the triceps from different angles, promoting overall arm development. Consistent triceps training not only enhances functional strength but also improves performance in various sports and daily activities. Embrace triceps workouts to achieve stronger, more sculpted arms and boost your overall fitness level.

FUNDAMENTAL TRICEPS MOVEMENTS

1)Triceps Dips

Triceps dips are a powerful bodyweight exercise that targets the triceps, shoulders, and chest. By supporting your body on parallel bars or sturdy surfaces, you lower and raise yourself using your arms, building strength and definition in your triceps. They're an effective addition to any upper body workout routine.




2)Close-Grip Bench Press

The Close-Grip Bench Press is a strength-training exercise that targets the triceps and chest muscles. With a narrower grip than the traditional bench press, it puts more emphasis on the triceps. This compound movement helps build upper body strength and enhances pressing power for various athletic activities.

4)Triceps pushdown

Triceps pushdown is a popular strength-training exercise that targets the triceps muscles. Using a cable machine and a straight bar or rope attachment, you extend your arms downward against resistance, engaging and sculpting the triceps. It's effective for building upper arm strength and enhancing overall arm aesthetics.




5)Diamond Push-up

Diamond push-ups, also known as triangle push-ups, are a challenging variation of the classic push-up. By forming a diamond shape with your hands, placing them close together beneath your chest, this exercise intensely targets the triceps, shoulders, and chest muscles, promoting upper body strength and definition.

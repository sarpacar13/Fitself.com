WELCOME TO SHOULDER WORKOUTS!!

Shoulder workouts are important for overall upper body strength and stability. Strong shoulders can improve posture, reduce the risk of injury, and enhance athletic performance. Exercises such as overhead presses, lateral raises, and rear deltoid flies can help build and tone the muscles in the shoulders, leading to better overall fitness.

FUNDAMENTAL SHOULDER MOVEMENTS

1)Barbell Shoulder Press

Barbell shoulder press is an effective strength-building exercise that targets the shoulders, triceps, and upper back. With proper form, it can improve upper body strength and stability, as well as have functional benefits for daily activities. It's important to start with lighter weights and use proper form to avoid injury.




2)Dumbell Shoulder Press

The dumbbell shoulder press is a compound exercise that targets the shoulders, triceps, and upper back. It involves pressing dumbbells overhead while sitting or standing. This exercise can improve upper body strength, balance, and coordination. It's important to start with lighter weights and use proper form to avoid injury.




3)Lateral Raise

The lateral raise is a popular shoulder exercise that targets the deltoid muscles. To perform it, stand with dumbbells by your sides and raise your arms out to the sides until they are parallel to the ground. This exercise helps strengthen and sculpt the shoulders, improving overall upper body aesthetics.




4)Rear Delt Fly

The rear delt fly is an exercise that targets the posterior deltoid muscles, which are located at the back of the shoulders. To perform it, bend forward at the waist, keep your arms slightly bent, and lift weights out to the sides, squeezing the shoulder blades together. This exercise helps improve shoulder posture and strengthens the rear deltoids.




5)Arnold Press

The Arnold press is a variation of the shoulder press named after Arnold Schwarzenegger. It involves holding dumbbells at shoulder level with palms facing towards you. As you press the weights overhead, rotate your palms outward. This exercise engages multiple shoulder muscles, providing a comprehensive shoulder workout and promoting muscle growth and strength.




6)Upright Row

The upright row is a shoulder exercise that targets the deltoids, traps, and upper back muscles. To perform it, hold a barbell or dumbbells with an overhand grip, and lift the weight upward, close to your body, until your elbows reach shoulder height. This exercise helps develop strong and defined shoulders and upper back muscles.
